# Governance & Compliance

Governance & Compliance professionals ensure that systems meet regulatory requirements, security standards, and organizational policies. They focus on risk management, audit readiness, and maintaining compliance certifications.

## Characteristics

### Primary Focus
- **Regulatory Compliance**: Meeting legal and industry requirements
- **Risk Management**: Identifying and mitigating risks
- **Audit Preparation**: Maintaining evidence and documentation
- **Policy Enforcement**: Ensuring adherence to standards
- **Security Oversight**: Monitoring security controls

### Key Responsibilities
- Conduct compliance assessments
- Manage risk registers and mitigation plans
- Prepare for audits and certifications
- Review security controls and evidence
- Document compliance mappings
- Report on compliance status

### Information Needs
- **Control matrices** mapping to standards
- **Compliance reports** and evidence
- **Risk assessments** and mitigation strategies
- **Audit procedures** and checklists
- **Policy documentation** and enforcement
- **Gap analysis** and remediation plans

### Communication Style
- Prefers structured reports and documentation
- Values evidence and audit trails
- Appreciates control mappings and matrices
- Responds well to risk-based frameworks

## Specialized Roles

### [Compliance Officer](./compliance-officer.md)

### [Auditor](./auditor.md)

### [Risk Manager](./risk-manager.md)

### [Data Protection Officer (DPO)](./dpo.md)
